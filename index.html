Tech Valley Ruby Brigade

Ruby enthusiasts from the Albany, Troy, Schenectady and Saratoga Springs, NY area

Who?

We are a group of Ruby enthusiasts from the Albany, Troy, Schenectady and Saratoga Springs, NY area.

What?

We talk about Ruby, Rails, and open source software. It's as simple as that.

When?

Meetings are held on the 4th Wednesday of each month. They start at 6pm and usually go until 8 or 8:30pm. Pizza and beer are provided.

Where?

Meetings are hosted at Enable Labs - 415 River Street, 4th Floor, Troy, NY 12180, Telephone: 866.895.8189

Why?

We Ruby and we want you to Ruby too!
