Overview

Who?

We are a group of Ruby enthusiasts from the Albany, Troy, Schenectady and Saratoga Springs, NY area.

What?

We talk about Ruby, Rails, open source software and it's as simple as that.

Where?

Meetings are hosted at Enable Labs - 415 River Street, 4th Floor, Troy, NY 12180, Telephone: 518.272.4455


View Larger Map

When?

Unless otherwise indicated, our meetings are held on the 4th Wednesday of each month. Meetings start at 6 PM and usually go until 8 or 8:30 PM. Pizza and beer are provided.

Why?

We love Ruby and we want you to love Ruby too!
